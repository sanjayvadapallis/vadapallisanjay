    Welcome to the Andhranatyam & Perini Dancers Fraternity

    Meet our accomplished and qualified Andhranatyam and Perini dancers across the globe. The order of appearance of dancers on this page has no bearing on the seniority, experience or contributions. We are updating this page on a very regular basis, so make sure you visit us back here to check our Andhranatyam and Perini dancers fraternity.

    Archana Upamaka

    Andhranatyam Dancer, Teacher & Choreographer

    St. Louis, Missouri

    Archana Upamaka is an Andhra Natyam performer and teacher. She trained under Guru Sri Nataraja Ramakrishna garu and his disciples Guru Sri Perini Ramesh garu, Guru Sri Perini Kumar garu and Guru Sri Kala Krishna garu. Archana debuted with her Arangetram in 1985, and performed Navajanardhana Parijatham a few years later.

    Nisha Maguluru

    Andhranatyam Dancer, Teacher & Choreographer

    St. Louis, Missouri

    Nisha has been practicing Andhra Natyam since 1988. She obtained her diploma from Telugu University in 1999 and has been teaching since 2008. With desire to continue the revival of the ancient dance and visceral that Andhra Natyam naturally enhances the being and persona of the practitioner, she founded Radhanika. Nisha works as a Senior Information Technology Manager in OFallon, MO.

    Satwika Penna

    Andhranatyam & Perini Dancer, Teacher & Choreographer

    Hyderabad, India

    Satwika Penna, is a disciple of Guru Kalakrishna, and daughter and disciple of Dr Suvarchala Suresh. An aeronautical engineer by education but a full time dancer holding Masters degree in Andhranatyam. University. She is the first Andhranatyam dancer to be selected as the recipient of Young Talent award by the Ministry of Culture, Govt of India. A charming performer who wishes to reach out to a varied audience and make them experience the true essence of these beautiful dance forms.

    Sanjay Vadapalli

    Andhranatyam & Perini Dancer & Senior Vice President, HSBC

    Hongkong

    Sanjay Vadapalli is a well-known Indian Classical Dancer, Performer, and Choreographer. He has mastered and performs Andhra Natyam, Perini (both lasyam and tandavam) and Navajanardhana Parijatam, ancient temple dance forms of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Sanjay exhibits good command over Technique (Nritta and Nritya) and Abhinaya and has won accolades across the art world. He is also well versed with the theory of dance and has successfully completed a Diploma in Classical Dance.
